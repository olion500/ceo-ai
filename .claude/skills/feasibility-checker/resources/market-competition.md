# Market & Competition Analysis

Detailed guide for researching market viability and competition.

## Overview

**CRITICAL:** Always use WebSearch to research competitors. Market validation requires real data, not assumptions.

### Competitive Landscape Matrix

**Example: Email Newsletter Tools Competitive Analysis**

| Competitor | Pricing | Users/Revenue | Strengths | Weaknesses | Source |
|------------|---------|---------------|-----------|------------|--------|
| Beehiiv | $49-99/mo | $15M ARR (2024) | Built for growth, referrals | Expensive, complex | indiehackers.com |
| ConvertKit | $25-50/mo | $29M ARR | Creator-focused | UI outdated | crunchbase.com |
| Buttondown | $9/mo | <$1M ARR | Simple, affordable | Limited features | twitter.com/jmduke |
| Substack | Free + 10% | $150M+ ARR | Network effects | Takes revenue cut | techcrunch.com |
| Ghost | $9-199/mo | $4M ARR | Open source | Technical setup | ghost.org/about |

**Key Insights from Example:**
- Price range: $0-99/month (market accepts wide range)
- Most successful have creator networks (Substack, ConvertKit, Beehiiv)
- Gap identified: Simple + affordable + growth features = Opportunity
- Revenue potential: Multiple companies >$1M ARR = Validated market

**Analyze by Type:**
- **Direct competitors:** Same solution, same target (Beehiiv, ConvertKit)
- **Indirect competitors:** Different solution, same problem (Substack)
- **Adjacent products:** Related problems (WordPress, Medium)

### Market Gaps Identification

From competitor research:
- What do all competitors miss?
- What do customers complain about?
- What features are table stakes?
- What would differentiate you?

## Revenue Model Analysis

### Short-term Revenue (Week 1-4)

**If market exists (competitors found):**
- Market size estimate (from competitor data)
- Proof of payment (competitor pricing)
- Distribution channels (where competitors get users)
- Week 1 realistic revenue
- Month 1 realistic revenue

**If no market exists (blue ocean):**
- ⚠️ HIGH RISK - need validation first
- Required validation:
  - 10+ customer interviews
  - 5+ pre-sales commitments
  - Landing page validation (100+ signups)

### Long-term Revenue (6-12 months)

**Similar Product Benchmarks:**

Research 3-5 similar products:
- Month 1 revenue
- Month 6 revenue
- Month 12 revenue
- Growth rate
- Sources for data

**Your Projections:**

Base on benchmark data:
- Conservative case (70% confidence)
- Base case (50% confidence)
- Optimistic case (20% confidence)

Include evidence-based reasoning.

## Market Validation Checklist

Before finalizing:
- [ ] Found 3+ direct competitors
- [ ] Analyzed competitor pricing
- [ ] Read 50+ competitor reviews
- [ ] Identified clear market gaps
- [ ] Validated revenue potential
- [ ] Talked to 10+ potential customers
- [ ] Confirmed willingness to pay

**Red flags:**
- No competitors AND no clear market
- All competitors failed/shut down
- Market declining
- Unclear monetization

**Green flags:**
- 3-10 competitors with revenue
- Growing market
- Clear pricing models
- Customer complaints you can solve
