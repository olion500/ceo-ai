## The Story

### Problem Discovery

Marc Lou identified a recurring pain point from his own experience building multiple SaaS products: developers waste weeks setting up the same boilerplate code (authentication, payments, email, deployment) before building their actual product.

**The Insight:** Most indie hackers and solo developers want to ship fast and validate ideas quickly, but get stuck in infrastructure setup. A NextJS boilerplate with all common integrations pre-configured would save weeks of development time.

**Market Validation:** Marc had already built multiple products himself and experienced this friction repeatedly. He was solving his own problem.
