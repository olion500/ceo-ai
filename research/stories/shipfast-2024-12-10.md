# ShipFast Success Story


## The Story

### Problem Discovery

Marc Lou had shipped 16 products before ShipFast (later 21 total by end of 2023). He experienced a recurring frustration:

**The Problem:**
Every time he started a new project, he spent weeks rebuilding the same boring infrastructure:
- Authentication systems
- Payment integration (Stripe)
- Database setup
- Email systems
- SEO basics
- Landing page boilerplate

**Time Wasted:** Weeks of repetitive setup before building actual product features

**His Insight:**
> "Make something people want" - Instead of chasing AI trends, solve a problem he personally experienced every time he built something.

**Validation:**
- He personally needed this for faster shipping
- Talked to other indie hackers with same pain
- Saw others building similar boilerplates but none felt complete
- Developer tools market was proven (Next.js, Vercel popular)

### MVP Development

**Timeline:** 1 week to build initial version

**Initial Features:**
- NextJS boilerplate code
- Stripe payment integration (one-time + subscription)
- MongoDB database setup
- Authentication (email, Google, magic links)
- Email system integration
- SEO-optimized structure
- Landing page template
- Documentation

**Tech Stack:**
- Framework: Next.js (React)
- Database: MongoDB
- Payment: Stripe
- Auth: NextAuth or similar
- Hosting: Vercel (likely)
- Language: JavaScript/TypeScript

**Development Process:**
- Built in 1 week
- Used his own experience from 16 previous products
- "No expectations" going in
- Focused on what he personally needed

**Initial Investment:** $0

**Development Philosophy:**
After previous products (MakeLanding, WorkbookPDF) had low conversions and angry customers, Marc adopted a new approach:
> "Make something people want" - Build what you actually need, not what's trendy.

### Key Decisions

1. **Build What He Personally Needed**
   - **Why:** Previous products (AI-driven) failed, this solved his real problem
   - **Result:** Perfect product-market fit from day 1

2. **Price at $200+ (Premium Positioning)**
   - **Why:** Reflects weeks of time saved, worth far more than cost
   - **Result:** Strong margins, serious customers

3. **Focus on Design/Landing Page**
   - **Why:** First impression matters enormously for conversions
   - **Result:** Landing page consistently praised, high conversion rate

4. **One Week Build Time (Not Months)**
   - **Why:** Speed to market, test hypothesis quickly
   - **Result:** Validated idea fast, beat competitors to market

5. **Stay Solo (No Employees)**
   - **Why:** High margins, full control, less complexity
   - **Result:** 90%+ profit margins, no team management overhead
