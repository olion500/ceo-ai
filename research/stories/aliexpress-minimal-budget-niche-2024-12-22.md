### Tactic 4: AliExpress Direct Sourcing

**What They Did:**

- Used AliExpress as exclusive product source
- No inventory investment
- Pay-per-order model
- Automated fulfillment

**How They Implemented:**

1. Browsed AliExpress for niche products
2. Ordered 3 samples ($5) to verify quality
3. Listed products on store with markup
4. Forwarded orders to AliExpress suppliers
5. Suppliers shipped directly to customers

**Context:**

- Zero inventory risk
- No upfront product costs
- Scalable without capital

**Results Achieved:**

- $25,000 in sales without holding inventory
- Automated order fulfillment
- Capital-efficient model

**Time Investment:**

- Product research: 2-4 hours
- Order processing: 5-10 minutes per order

**Cost:**

- Sample orders: $5
- Product costs: 40-50% of sale price (paid after customer payment)
