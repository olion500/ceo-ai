## The Ultimate "Build for Yourself" Story

Pieter Levels is the quintessential indie hacker who built products from his laptop as a digital nomad, solving problems he personally faced while traveling the world.

---

## Product Portfolio

### 1. Nomad List (2014) - Flagship Product

**Personal Problem:**
- As digital nomad, needed to find best cities to live/work
- No centralized data on cost of living, internet speed, weather, safety
- Hard to compare cities for remote work

**Solution:**
- Built database of cities ranked for digital nomads
- Community-driven data and reviews
- Membership platform for nomad community

**Current Status:**
- Millions of users monthly
- $20K-$40K/month revenue historically
- $1.5M+ annual recurring revenue (2024)
- Thousands of paying members

### 2. Remote OK - Job Board

**Personal Problem:**
- Needed remote job opportunities as digital nomad
- Existing job boards not focused on remote work

**Solution:**
- Remote job board specifically for remote positions
- Automated job aggregation
- Premium job postings for companies

**Revenue:**
- Part of overall portfolio ($3M/year total)

### 3. Photo AI (2023) - AI Breakthrough

**Personal Problem:**
- Wanted AI-generated photos of himself
- Existing services too expensive or complicated

**Solution:**
- AI headshot generation service
- Train model on your photos
- Generate professional photos with AI

**Explosive Growth:**
- **Week 1:** $150,000 revenue
- **18 months:** Six-figure monthly revenue
- **Current:** $132,000/month
- Fastest-growing product in portfolio

### 4. Avatar AI

**Revenue:** $100K+ within months of launch

## Building Philosophy

### Zero Employees Philosophy

> "I make $3 million per year with zero employees" - Pieter Levels

**Why No Employees:**
- Maintains full control
- Higher profit margins
- Faster decision-making
- No management overhead
- Pure profit after costs

### Tech Stack Philosophy

**Uses "Boring" Technology:**
- Vanilla PHP (not frameworks)
- jQuery (not React/Vue)
- SQLite (not PostgreSQL)
- Simple VPS hosting

**Why:**
- Proven and stable
- Fast to develop
- Easy to maintain alone
- Don't need cutting-edge for revenue
- Focus on product, not technology

## Pieter's Advice to Indie Hackers
