## Founder's Lessons & Reflections

### What Worked

- "Stumbled upon winning product while browsing AliExpress"
- £26 → £56 pricing (115% markup) worked for 18+ months
- 1 sale/day consistency achievable as side hustle
- International markets (5 countries) increase volume
- 20+ ad sets needed to find winners

### What Didn't Work (Inferred)

- Systematic product research (didn't use, relied on browsing)
- Scaling beyond £400/month (not attempted, satisfied with side income)
- Organic-only marketing (needed paid ads for consistency)

### Advice to Others (Inferred)

- Browse AliExpress for products you'd personally buy
- Test high markups (100%+) in emotional niches (toys, gifts)
- Budget £400 for ad testing (20+ ad sets)
- Use Canva for free content creation
- Multi-currency stores increase addressable market
- Side gig approach (£400/month) is viable long-term
- 1 sale/day is achievable and sustainable

**Mindset lessons:**

- Side income focus reduces pressure
- Serendipity works (casual browsing found winner)
- Consistency > growth (18 months at £400/month)
- High margins enable profitability at low volume
